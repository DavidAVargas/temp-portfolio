About Me

David A Vargas is a versatile Full-Stack Developer currently freelancing on Fiverr and Upwork. With a strong focus on the MERN stack and PostgreSQL, he combines his skills in both front-end and back-end development to build robust, scalable applications. David is proficient in JavaScript, Python, and web development frameworks, allowing him to tackle diverse technical challenges. His unique journey blends patient care with software development, showcasing his passion for innovative solutions and teamwork. David is active in the coding community, regularly attending meetups and engaging with fellow developers. Outside of coding, he enjoys traveling, reading about business and self-improvement, and embracing creativity. David’s goal is to craft sites and applications that are not only cutting-edge but also genuinely useful for users. He is eager to land his first full-time role in software development, where he can apply his skills and continue to grow in a collaborative and forward-thinking environment.
